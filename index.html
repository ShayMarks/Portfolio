About Me

Hey! I'm Shay Marks. I am an honors graduate (with distinction) in Software Engineering, specialized in Full-Stack development. Currently, I work as a Manual QA Tester, while maintaining a deep passion for discovering innovative ideas and researching cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence. I thrive on the entire creative process—from architecture to beautifully crafted, user-centric interfaces.

Since childhood, I've had an insatiable curiosity for how software, websites, and games operate under the hood. This drove me into programming and independent research, turning questions into robust digital solutions.

I am an avid self-learner. When passion meets code, independent learning becomes an exciting journey rather than a task. On this portfolio, I showcase projects born from a desire to solve real problems, have fun, and conquer technical challenges (and inevitably, squash complex bugs).

Beyond the IDE, I'm deeply creative. I compose my own music and leverage AI tools for track production, which I share on YouTube. I also enjoy tactical games like chess, swimming, and immersive online gaming.

If you are looking for a dedicated, creative developer who codes with profound passion—I'm here. Let's build something phenomenal.
